Syracuse • Focus Mode

Study Routine Guide

A simple, high-focus plan at Syracuse University

Steps

1) Checklist of belongings

Gather everything needed: laptop, charger, notebook, student ID, headphones, water, snacks.

2) Pick up food at Schine

Head to Schine Student Center. Choose a quick meal that sustains energy.

3) Add a caffeine boost

Grab coffee from Dunkin’ or an energy drink if you prefer.

4) Go to Bird Library

Bird offers quiet space to focus and avoid distractions.

5) Head downstairs

Use the underground level to eat and start assignments.

6) Make an assignments checklist

Write today’s tasks. Build accountability.

7) Move to a quiet floor

Pick a seat on an upper floor where it is quieter.

8) Phone far away

Put your phone in your bag. Enable Do Not Disturb.

9) Use the 25/5 routine

Work 25 minutes. Break 5 minutes. Repeat.

10) Pack up

Checklist complete. Pack and head home.
