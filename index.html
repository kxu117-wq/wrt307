Syracuse University Study Instructions

Purpose: study efficiently away from home
Where to go
Pick places that reduce distractions
How to study
Follow simple steps and produce output
How long
Use 25/5 cadence and finish the list
  1. 1

    Checklist of belongings

    Pack your study kit

    ID, laptop and charger, power strip, headphones, textbooks, notebook, water, snack.

    Image of Schine Student Center

    Pick simple fuel. Avoid heavy or high-sugar options.

  3. 3

    Short video of the food court

    Add a caffeine boost

    Choose a small coffee or energy drink to stay alert.

  4. 4

    Map / Bird Library photos

    Move to Bird Library

    Choose a quiet zone to lower distractions.

  5. 5

    Route video (door → underground)

    Eat downstairs first

    Use the lower-level dining area. Plan while you eat.

  6. 6

    Assignments checklist photo

    Make a short task list

    List 3–5 doable items. Order by priority.

  7. 7

    Open area on upper floors

    Move up to a quiet floor

    Choose a calmer space with more open seats.

  8. 8

    Phone out of reach

    Hide your phone

    Backpack + notifications off. Remove temptation.

  9. 9

    25 min work / 5 min break

    Run the 25/5 cadence

    After 4 cycles, take a 15–20 min break. Check off tasks.

  10. 10

    Belongings + finished work

    Review and wrap up

    Tick completed items. Note blockers and next steps. Pack and head home.
