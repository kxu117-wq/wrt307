Syracuse University Study Instructions

Purpose: where to study, how to study, how long. Simple 10-step flow.
Step 1
Belongings checklist

Pack ID, laptop, charger, headphones, notebook, water, snack.

Step 2
Go to Schine Student Center

Pick simple food. Avoid heavy or high-sugar options.

Step 3
Grab a caffeine boost

Small coffee or energy drink to stay alert.

Step 4
Walk to Bird Library

Choose a quiet zone to reduce distractions.

Step 5
Go downstairs at Bird

Eat on the lower level and outline tasks.

Step 6
Make your assignment checklist

List 3–5 doable items in priority order.

Step 7
Move to a top floor

Quieter space with more open seats.

Step 8
Stow your phone

Backpack + notifications off to remove temptation.

Step 9
Use the 25/5 routine

Work 25 minutes. Break 5. After 4 cycles, rest 15–20.

Step 10
Pack up and head home

Check off the list. Note blockers and next steps.
