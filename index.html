Study Routine Guide

A simple plan for focused work at Syracuse University

Steps

1) Checklist of belongings

Gather everything needed for your study session: laptop, charger, notebook, student ID, headphones, water, and snacks.

2) Pick up food at Schine

Head to Schine Student Center. Choose a quick meal that will keep you energized.

3) Add a caffeine boost

Grab coffee from Dunkin' or an energy drink if you prefer.

4) Go to Bird Library

Bird is a quiet place to focus and avoid distractions.

5) Head downstairs

Go to the underground level where you can eat and get started on assignments.

6) Make an assignments checklist

Write down what you will complete today. This builds accountability.

7) Move to a quiet floor

Find a seat on one of the upper floors where it is usually quieter.

8) Phone far away

Put your phone in your backpack so it is not accessible during study.

9) Use the 25/5 routine

Work for 25 minutes. Take a 5 minute break. Repeat until finished.

10) Pack up

When your checklist is complete, pack your belongings and head home.
